## About the Course

A practically-oriented course that prepares future business leaders to make informed decisions using data. No advanced mathematics required вЂ” students master **Excel** and **Power BI** through real business case studies. From customer behaviour analysis to strategic planning, the course develops the critical thinking and analytical skills essential for today's data-driven business environment.

Assessment is based on participation, projects, and practical tasks вЂ” no traditional written exam.

**Prerequisites:** Basic Excel skills and high-school level algebra.
