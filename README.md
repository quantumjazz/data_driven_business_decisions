# Data-Driven Business Decisions (BUBB)

**Вземане на бизнес решения на базата на данни**
New Bulgarian University · Department of Administration and Management
Assoc. Prof. Dr. Viktor Avramov · vavramov@nbu.bg

---

## About the Course

A practically-oriented course that prepares future business leaders to make informed decisions using data. No advanced mathematics required — students master **Excel** and **Power BI** through real business case studies. From customer behaviour analysis to strategic planning, the course develops the critical thinking and analytical skills essential for today's data-driven business environment.

Assessment is based on participation, projects, and practical tasks — no traditional written exam.

**Prerequisites:** Basic Excel skills and high-school level algebra.

---

## Course Modules

| Module | Topics | Weeks |
|--------|--------|-------|
| 1. Why Data Matters in Business | The cost of bad decisions · A journey into data | 1–2 |
| 2. Business Analysis Through Data | Storytelling with numbers · Models · Dashboards | 3–5 |
| 3. Forecasting the Future | Forecasting · Customer behaviour · Risk | 6–8 |
| 4. Making Better Decisions | Optimisation · A/B testing · Game theory | 9–11 |
| 5. Business Analytics in Action | Communication · Ethics · Your analytics toolkit | 12–14 |
| **Final** | **Project presentations** | **15** |
